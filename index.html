INTRODUCTION

I am a Master's student in Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Tech where I work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Aerospace Systems Design Lab. I received my B.S. in Aerospace Engineering from Georgia Tech in May 2019. Throughout my time studying at Georgia Tech, I have had the chance to gain experience in the field of Aerospace Engineering through classwork, research, projects, and internships related to both Space and Aviation.

My main interest within Aerospace Engineering is working on the development of disruptive aerospace systems or technologies that have the capability to sustainably revolutionize their respective domains. While this is not restricted to any specific sector of the aerospace industy, I am particularly drawn to the design of aerospace systems that facilitate access to space and systems that work to enable a sustainable human presence beyond Earth. In addition, I have a strong interest in concepts such as Urban Air Mobility and Super/Hypersonic flight that aim to facilitate the transport of cargo and/or passengers within and between cities.

This web page was created to provide more in-depth details regarding the experience I have gained and the projects I have contributed to that may not otherwise be captured in my resume.
