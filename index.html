INTRODUCTION

I am a Master's student in Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Tech where I work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Aerospace Systems Design Lab. Throughout my time studying at Georgia Tech, I have had the chance to gain valuable experience in the field of Aerospace Engineering through classwork, research, projects, and internships related to both Space and Aviation.

My main interest within Aerospace Engineering is the development of aerospace systems that have the capability to sustainably revolutionize their respective domains. While this is not restricted to any sub-domain within the aerospace industy, I am particularly drawn to the design of aerospace systems that democratize access to space and that work to enable a sustainable human presence beyond Earth. In addition, I have a strong interest in concepts such as Urban Air Mobility that aim to facilitate the transport of goods and passengers within and between cities. Beyond Aerospace Engineering, I spend my time playing soccer, following autosports, developing my computer science skills, and working towards my private pilot license.

This web page was created to provide more in-depth details regarding the experience I have gained that may not otherwise be captured in my resume.
