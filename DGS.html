Delta Global Services

Role: Pilot Training Service Intern
June 2016 - August 2016

Summary :

Delta Global Services (DGS) is a wholly owned subsidiary of Delta Airlines which handles many of the activities necessary to support the airline's operation. Within DGS, I worked in the Pilot Training Service (PTS) where my work was focused on a variety of analytical tasks.

My Contribution :

  • Developed and implemented a scorecard which uses various metrics tracked by PTS to give an insight into PTS's performance and efficiency.
  • Worked on a supplier diversity initiative which focused on categorizing existing suppliers and identifying opportunities to increase spending on diverse-eligible suppliers.
  • Performed data collection and processing to reflect the utilization of aicraft simulators used for pilot training.
