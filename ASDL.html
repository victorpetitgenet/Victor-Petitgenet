Summary :

In addition to completing the required Master's coursework, I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistance (GRA) at the Aerospace Systems Design Lab (ASDL). Working as a GRA at the ASDL entails participating in deliverable-focused projects with a high level of collaboration and interaction with industry partners.

The project I am working on as a GRA is sponsored by the Aviation Sustainability Center (ASCENT), an entity funded by NASA and the FAA among others. This project is focused on a wide variety of tasks revolving around the study and design of a supersonic commercial aircraft for the near future. The work I have been completing as part of this project is focused on aerodynamics.
